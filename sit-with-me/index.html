Sit With Me

A simple meditation timer that includes scheduled bell sounds.

Sit With Me is a simple meditation app with a timer feature as well as a scheduled bells feature. The timer lets you time your meditation. It includes bell sounds and a handful of ambient sounds. The bells feature allows you to set bell sounds at regular intervals.

This is the second iOS app I've worked on, and my first solo app, so if you have suggestions for improvements, please let me know! I hope to include more bell sounds as well as improve on the quality of the sounds in future releases.
