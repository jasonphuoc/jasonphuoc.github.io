About Jason

I'm a recent graduate of UCLA with an M.S. in computer science. Before attending graduate school fulltime, I worked at the L.A. Times for over eight years as a Senior Editor for Digital. I kept an eye on the website content management system, made websites and webpages, trained journalists on how to use various technologies and did a little bit of writing. Before the L.A. Times, I worked for Yahoo! News. As for my undergraduate learnings, I earned a B.A. in history from Yale.

I enjoy expressing ideas and making things, whether through words and photos (above, a photo of Bryce Canyon National Park I captured during the winter) or lines of code. Mostly the latter nowadays.
